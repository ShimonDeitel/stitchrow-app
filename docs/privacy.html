Privacy Policy

Stitch Row - Cross Stitch Log is an offline-first app. All data you enter — projects, notes, and photos you attach — is stored locally on your device in your device's Application Support storage. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your data. There are no analytics SDKs, no ad networks, and no network calls made by this app beyond the App Store's own purchase and restore-purchase flows, which are handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework.

If you delete the app, your local data is deleted with it. We recommend using your device's backup features (such as iCloud device backup) if you want to preserve your data.
